Over the last few years, there has been an uptick in “smart-devices” that are placed in users’ homes that allow the user to keep track of various different details in the users’ environment, from the humidity of their home to the temperature of their home. There have been vendors who sell various different “systems” but these systems lack flexibility in terms of specific types of setups or they require additional hardware to be purchased along with the system.
