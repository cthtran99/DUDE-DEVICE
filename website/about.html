OUR STORY

As founders of the DUDE device, we seek to create an easy experience for those in need of a device that can simply track and record data. Our shop contains everything needed for a mobile sensor device, making it more convenient and very user-focused. From shopping for parts to reviewing data logs, here at DUDE we have everything for your surveying needs.
